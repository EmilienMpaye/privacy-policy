Short Version

Numbera does not collect any personal information directly. The only data activity in this app comes from Google AdMob, which serves ads and may collect device-level identifiers as described below.

About This App

Numbera is an educational math game developed by HappyLeaf Studio. The app is designed to help players practice arithmetic through engaging, fun game worlds.

This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled when you use Numbera on Android.

Data We Collect

HappyLeaf Studio does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data such as your name, email address, location, or account information.

All game progress (coins, levels, power-ups) is stored locally on your device only and is never sent to any server.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

Numbera uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use the following to serve relevant ads:

  • Advertising ID (Android Ad ID)
  • Device model and operating system version
  • IP address (used to infer approximate location for ad targeting)
  • App interaction data (e.g. which ads were shown or tapped)
  • Crash and diagnostics information

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by HappyLeaf Studio. You can review Google's practices and opt out of personalized ads through your device settings:

Android: Settings → Google → Ads → "Delete advertising ID" or "Opt out of Ads Personalization"

Children's Privacy

Numbera is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app uses non-personalized ads where required by applicable law (such as COPPA in the United States).

If you believe your child has provided personal information through this app, please contact us and we will address it promptly.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party service integrated in Numbera is Google AdMob. No analytics platforms, social SDKs, or data brokers are used.

Data Retention

Since HappyLeaf Studio does not collect or store any user data on external servers, there is no data retention on our end. Any data collected by AdMob is governed by Google's own retention policies.

Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights regarding personal data including access, correction, or deletion. Since we do not hold any personal data ourselves, any such requests related to ad data should be directed to Google.

You can manage your ad preferences and data directly through your Google account or Android device settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
